TRIANGLE TRACKER

There are six types of triangles, 3 in relation to sides and 3 to angles. In this page, I focused on those in relation to sides. These are ;

  1. Equilateral
  2. Isosceles
  3. Scalene

The page will help you identify what type of a triangle it is by clicking in the data box space and add the length of each size below. It will also help you know whether your figure is a triangle or not .
